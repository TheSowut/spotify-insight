###### a minimalistic app that showcases your most listened monthly Spotify tracks

## How to use:
<p style='margin-bottom: 5px'>1. Obtain an access token by logging in https://developer.spotify.com and copying your token.</p>
<img src="images/access_token.png" alt="spotify developers website" style="width: 75%">
<p style='margin-bottom: 5px'>2. Use your access token inside the app.</p>
<img src="images/login_screen.PNG" alt="application login screen" style="width: 75%">
<p>3. You're all done, <i>enjoy</i>.</p>
